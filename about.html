Our Story

Joseph Sea Foods is one of the leading suppliers and exporters of quality sea food products in Thoothukudi. With more than 25 years of experience in the sea food industry, we sell our ready-to-fry sea food products to a variety of clients including food companies, retail chains, restaurants, club stores and distributors spread across the developed markets of Tamil Nadu.

Our Mission

The goal of our company is to exceed the expectations of our customers consistently through innovation, commitment, and fair dealing with the environmental issues.By establishing good, reliable, and mutually beneficial relationships, we provide our customers with high-quality, straightforward, and sustainable fish products.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to provide our customers with high-quality, healthy, and ethical fish products. Creating good relationships with staff, but also with clients and suppliers, is at the heart of how we operate. Furthermore, we believe it is important for our company to contribute to sustainable and socially responsible initiatives. As a company, we are committed to a value system of honesty, integrity, and transparency that we practice religiously with all stakeholders. Our main goal is to satisfy our customers and never compromise on the quality of our products. Whatever we do, we hope to make a difference by creating value. We thrive on a challenge and strive for perfection.
